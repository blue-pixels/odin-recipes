Lasagna

Description

This recipe has simple ingredients but tons of flavor with a creamy ricotta mixture, savory meat sauce, and lots of mozzarella cheese.

This is a great freezer meal, make-ahead recipe, and leftovers freeze and reheat super well.

ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles (uncooked or pre-boiled)
  • Ground beef and Italian sausage
  • Onion and garlic
  • Canned crushed tomatoes or pasta sauce
  • Tomato paste
  • Italian seasoning
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Eggs
  • Parsley
  • Mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • Parmesan cheese, shredded

Steps

  1. Cook the lasagna noodles until al dente, then drain and rinse under cold water
  2. In a skillet, brown the ground beef, sausage, onion and garlic. Drain any excess fat
  3. Add crushed tomatoes or pasta sauce, tomato paste, Italian seasoning, salt and pepper. Simmer for 5 minutes
  4. In a bowl, mix the ricotta cheese, parsley, egg, salt and 1 1/2 cups of the mozzarella cheese
  5. Layer the meat sauce, ricotta mixture and lasagna noodles in a baking dish, repeating the layers
  6. Top with remaining mozzarella and parmesan cheeses
  7. Cover and bake at 350Â°F for 45 minutes. Remove foil and bake 15 more minutes until browned and bubbly
  8. Let stand 15 minutes before serving
