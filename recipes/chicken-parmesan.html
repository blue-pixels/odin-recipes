Chicken Parmesan

Description

This easy chicken parmesan recipe features crispy breaded chicken breasts topped with marinara sauce and melty mozzarella cheese. It's a delicious Italian-inspired meal that's perfect for a weeknight dinner.

Ingredients

  • Boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • Breadcrumbs or panko
  • Grated Parmesan cheese
  • Eggs, beaten
  • Olive oil
  • Marinara sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • Fresh basil, chopped

Steps

  1. Pound chicken breasts to an even thickness between two sheets of plastic wrap or parchment paper.
  2. Set up a breading station with the beaten eggs in one bowl and the breadcrumbs mixed with Parmesan in another.
  3. Dip the chicken in the egg, then coat both sides with the breadcrumb mixture, pressing to adhere.
  4. In a large skillet, heat 1-2 tablespoons olive oil over medium heat.
  5. Cook the breaded chicken for 3-4 minutes per side until golden brown. Transfer to a baking dish.
  6. Top each chicken breast with a couple tablespoons marinara sauce and a handful of mozzarella cheese.
  7. Bake at 400Â°F for 15-20 minutes until chicken is cooked through and cheese is melted.
  8. Garnish with fresh chopped basil before serving.
